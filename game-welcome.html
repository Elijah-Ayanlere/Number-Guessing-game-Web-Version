Welcome, ! Thank you for joining the Unique Number Guessing Game. You're about to embark on an exhilarating adventure!

In this game, your mission is to guess a number that has been randomly selected within a specific range. Exciting, isn't it? Choose from different difficulty levels, each with its own unique set of rules.

Your goal is to make accurate guesses, and if you're feeling daring, you can even take on the Wildcard Round for a chance to earn bonus points. It's an optional challenge that adds an extra layer of excitement to the game.

To assist you on your journey, you have a special tool called the Hint Coin. This magical coin can provide you with a hint that might lead you to the correct answer. When you're ready to uncover a clue, simply click the "Use Hint Coin" button in the game.
